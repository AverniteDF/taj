[BIO]
<p><strong>Victor Connelly</strong> is a visionary transhumanist and speculative fiction author who contributes thought-provoking articles on the future of humanity in a world where technology and biology increasingly merge. With a deep interest in Futurism and the potential of human augmentation, Victor's work offers imaginative yet scientifically grounded explorations of what might lie ahead for our species. His writing often bridges the gap between hard science and speculative fiction, challenging readers to think beyond the present and consider the ethical, social, and biological implications of our technological evolution.</p>
