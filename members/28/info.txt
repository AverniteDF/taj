[BIO]
<p><strong>Dr. Nathan Chen</strong> is a theoretical physicist who passionately believes in the idea that our universe is fundamentally computational. With a Ph.D. in physics and a focus on quantum mechanics and information theory, Nathan contributes articles exploring the concept of the universe as a vast simulation. His work delves deep into the philosophical and scientific implications of this theory, touching on topics like digital physics, cellular automata, and the role of information as the fabric of reality. Nathan's articles challenge readers to think about the nature of existence and the possibility that our reality may be governed by algorithms.</p>
